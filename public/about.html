The Creators

Jenna Kornbluth


Jenna Kornbluth is a 7th grader living in Connecticut who coded this website. She became interested in aeroponics after she heard a friend talking about it.

Maya Hruskar


Maya Hruskar is a 7th grader living in Connecticut who originally had the idea for AerKids. She became interested in aeroponics because of her interest in biology.

James Cao


James Cao is a 7th grader living in Connecticut who helped build the prototype. He became interested in aeroponics after seeing a video about the alternate ways of farming.

The Idea

The idea for AerKids was created when a school teacher introduced the AT&T Invention Challenge. We all wanted to inspire kids to be more interested in science, and when we had this idea, we knew we wanted to make it a reality.
